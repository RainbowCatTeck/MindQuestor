⚖️ legal

privacy policy

mindquestor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. everything you add to the app — habits, quests, vision board images, settings — is stored entirely in your browser's localStorage. nothing is sent to any server. no account is needed to use mindquestor. there is no required login, no email, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

clearing your browser data will erase your mindquestor data. use the data backup section in settings to export a copy first.

❓ faq

what is mindquestor?

a free productivity app built by and for neurodivergent people — ADHD, autistic, chronically ill, disabled. no clinical tone, no dark patterns, no "just try harder".

does it cost anything?

mindquestor is free forever. no subscription, no premium tier, no hidden costs. if you want to support development you can buy us a coffee on ko-fi — but you never have to.

do i need an account?

no account is needed to get started — open the app and use it straight away. no email, no sign-up required.

where is my data?

everything stays in your browser's localStorage. nothing is sent anywhere. no server ever sees your data.

will it sync across devices?

not yet — sync is on the roadmap. for now, you can save the HTML file and open it on any device to carry your data with you.

does it work offline?

the web version needs an internet connection to load. once the page is open, nothing is fetched externally — the font and all assets are self-contained, so the app doesn't make any outside requests while you're using it.

full offline access is planned as a premium feature — we'll let you know when it's available.

what browsers work?

any modern browser — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge. works on desktop and mobile.

i found a bug

use the 💬 feedback form in the footer. select 🐛 bug and describe what happened — what you did, what you expected, and what actually happened. a screenshot helps if you can. you'll get a reply if you include your email.
